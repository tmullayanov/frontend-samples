Peter the Great

the Emperor of All Russia

Portrait by Paul Delaroche

    Here's the brief timeline of Peter's life:

  • 1672 - born in Moscow, Tsardom of Russia
  • 1700 - started Great Northern War against Sweden
  • 1703 - found the city of St.Petersburgh
  • 1721 - defeated Sweden in Great Northern War.
  • 1721 - was coronated as the Emperor of All Russia.
  • 1725 - died at the age of 52. As the legends say, his last words were "Leave all to...". And then, exhausted by the effort, he passed away.
